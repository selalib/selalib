sll_m_splines_pp Module Reference

Description

Splines in pp form.

Authors
Katharina Kormann, IPP Benedikt Perse, IPP

Implement splines on a uniform grid, periodic boundaries implemented for degree 0-6 and clamped boundaries for degree 1-3
