42  ! This is a simplistic initializer aimed at a 4d cartesian distribution
43  ! function, periodic in x and y, and compact-ish in vx and vy.
44  !
45  ! Basically:
46  !
47  ! f(x,y,vx,vy) = pi/(8*vt^2)*sin(pi*x)*sin(pi*y)*exp(-(vx^2+vy^2)/(2*vt^2))
48  !
49  ! It is meant to be used in the intervals:
50  ! x: [ 0,1]
51  ! y: [ 0,1]
52  ! vx: [-1,1]
53  ! vy: [-1,1]
54  ! thus vthermal has to be small relative to 1 to make the support as
55  ! 'compact' as possible.
56  !
57  ! Issue to resolve: to initialize the data we need the 'mesh'/'mapping'
58  ! information. How to deal with this in 4D? Do we pass multiple 2D
59  ! meshes? As something tentative, here we just write an ad hoc 4D
60  ! cartesian mesh.
